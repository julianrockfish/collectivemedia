Different Stages

1

Brief

You have an idea for something; a story to tell, an event coming up, a dream you want to make a reality.

2

Analysis

We collaborate with you on the idea to pre-visualize the best it can be.

3

Planning

We plan the crew and artists for the shoot, edit and post production along with a matching schedule. We believe the process needs to be an open book and tailored to meet any client deadline, meeting, review, etc.

4

Execution

We're off to the races and the excitement of production begins. We are driven by our ability to continually impress all client expectations.

Our team will not stop until we know both you and your client are impressed with the final product.

Production

Location Scouting, Casting, Lighting, Camera, Grip and permitting - all done by us for you. We plan every detail out so the shoot day(s) are as successful as they can be.

Editiorial

Matching the right creative editor to your project is key. We can edit on set for same day social media activations or with a schedule.

Design

Storyboarding and making stye frames are done to showcase the work and tweak the overall story and mood before we ever set foot on set.

Color

Shooting all the content raw is just how it goes these days. Don't sweat a thing - we color everything we do in post to make sure your product colors match, skin tones are perfect and the video has the right look.

VFX

From a simple power cable under a desk being removed because it makes the shot look cluttered to much more challenging things. VFX will be used "as needed" and planned for in advance. Been there - done that.

Audio

Voiceover, location audio clean-up and music make the final video come to life.
